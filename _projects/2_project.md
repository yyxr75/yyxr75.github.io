Sparse, noisy automotive-radar point clouds are enhanced toward LiDAR-like density using a diffusion prior guided by arbitrary, **unpaired** LiDAR data — removing the need for paired supervision.

Preprint: _Unsupervised Radar Point-Cloud Enhancement via Arbitrary LiDAR-Guided Diffusion Prior_ — arXiv:2505.09887 (Y. Yang, J. Liu, G. Luo, H. Li, E. Ahn, M. Rahimi Azghadi, T. Huang).
