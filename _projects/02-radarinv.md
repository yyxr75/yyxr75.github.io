Automotive radar point clouds are sparse and noisy. **RadarINV** enhances them toward LiDAR-like density using a diffusion prior guided by *arbitrary, unpaired* LiDAR data — removing the need for paired radar/LiDAR supervision.

Preprint: *Unsupervised Radar Point-Cloud Enhancement via Arbitrary LiDAR-Guided Diffusion Prior*.

[Code](https://github.com/yyxr75/RadarINV) &middot; [arXiv:2505.09887](https://arxiv.org/abs/2505.09887)
