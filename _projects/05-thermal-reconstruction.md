Master's research at Beijing Institute of Technology: designed the complete hardware, software, and mechanical pipeline for reconstructing 3D thermal surfaces of the human body, fusing thermal imagery with 3D geometry.

Published as *Fusion Reconstruction Method for 3D Temperature Fields on the Human Body Surface* in *Infrared Technology*, 2022.
