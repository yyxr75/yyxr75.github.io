Contributed to a James Cook University collaboration with the Queensland Government (DETSI) building a computer-vision system that distinguishes crocodiles from other floating objects.

Models were trained on thousands of hours of footage and deployed on a mobile, trailer-mounted camera platform for real-time public-safety monitoring in North Queensland.
