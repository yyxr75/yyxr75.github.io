**DeblurSDI** casts blind image deblurring as a _self-diffusion_ process that jointly recovers the sharp image and the unknown blur kernel from a single degraded input — with no pre-training and no external dataset.

- Blind, single-image setting: no clean/blurred pairs and no pre-trained prior required.
- Accepted to **CVPR 2026** (Y. Yang, G. Luo).
- Part of a broader line of work on self-diffusion for inverse problems (arXiv:2510.21417).
